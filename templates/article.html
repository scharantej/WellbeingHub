How to Cope with Anxiety

Anxiety is a common mental health condition that can cause feelings of worry, nervousness, and fear. It can be triggered by a variety of factors, including stress, life events, and genetics.

There are many ways to cope with anxiety, including:

  • Talking to a therapist or counselor
  • Taking medication
  • Practicing relaxation techniques
  • Getting regular exercise
  • Eating a healthy diet
